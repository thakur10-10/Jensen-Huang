Jensen Huang

Co-founder and CEO of NVIDIA

A visionary leader who shaped the world of graphics processing and AI.

About Jensen Huang

Born in Taiwan and raised in the United States, Jensen Huang co-founded NVIDIA in 1993. He revolutionized the graphics processing unit (GPU) industry, leading NVIDIA to become a major player in AI and gaming technology worldwide.

Career Highlights

  • Co-founded NVIDIA Corporation in 1993.
  • Introduced the first GPU in 1999, reshaping visual computing.
  • Expanded NVIDIA’s vision to AI, data centers, and autonomous vehicles.
  • Named one of Time’s 100 Most Influential People.
